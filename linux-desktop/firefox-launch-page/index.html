Welcome to Coursera's Virtual Linux Desktop

This browser is intended primarily for developmental purposes, to visualize any HTML or other web development projects. That being said, it can function as a browser in a limited capability.

When using this web browser, please be mindful of the following:

• Your instructor has only allowed browsing on a handful of websites; most websites will be blocked
• Please contact your course instructor or TA for this list of websites
• Whole websites or parts of websites, like images and links, may not load or work as expected if they're blocked
• Navigating to malicious, inappropriate, or crypto mining oriented websites can lead to an account ban, so please browse responsibly

Happy learning!
